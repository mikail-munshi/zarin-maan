Dear Zarin Ma'am,

Some teachers teach lessons. You taught us something far rarer — you made us feel the beauty of language. Every class you walked into, you brought with you a kind of quiet electricity that turned a regular lecture into something we actually looked forward to.

You had a gift for making Communicative English feel alive — not like a course we had to get through, but a world we were glad to step into. Your explanations were clear, your enthusiasm was contagious, and your warmth made every student in the room feel seen.

I will carry your lessons with me — not just the grammar and the expressions, but the way you showed up for us, class after class, with energy and care that never felt forced.

Wherever you go next, I hope you know that you left a mark on every student who sat in your classroom. You were not just a teacher — you were an inspiration.

With deep gratitude and warm wishes,
